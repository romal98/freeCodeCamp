USADA PEKORA

"Konoyaro!!"

Here's a time line of Usada Pekora:

    2019 :
  • 13 June 2019 - First announced
  • 07 July 2019 - During the hololive karaoke session, her name and Twitter account were officially announced alongside Uruha Rushia.
  • 28 May 2019 - Her Twitter account was opened while her YouTube channel was opened on 3 July. She debuted on YouTube with her introductory stream on 17 July.
    • 2020 :
  • 04 December 2020 - She reached 1 million YouTube subscribers during a stream being the fourth member of hololive to reach this milestone after Gawr Gura, Inugami Korone and Shirakami Fubuki and the third to do so on the Japanese branch. She is also the first member of hololive JP's 3rd Gen to reach it.
  • 13 December 2020 - She revealed her VTuber job to her mother live on a stream with over 120,000 viewers. Pekora previously had kept it a secret from her family, and had previously decided to reveal it upon reaching 1 million subscribers.
    • 2021
  • 06 March 2021 - Pekorandom Brain! surpassed 1,000,000 views.
  • 02 June 2021 - She reached 1,500,000 YouTube subscribers during a singing endurance stream. This makes her the 3rd member of hololive to achieve this milestone, as well as the 2nd member of hololive JP. She is also the first member of hololive JP's 3rd Gen to achieve it.
    • 2022
  • 16 July 2022 - She debuted new accessories for her main costume. Pekora can now use swords, water guns and hamburgers in her hair instead of her usual carrots.
  • 05 August 2022 - Pekora reached 2,000,000 YouTube subscribers during a karaoke stream, making her the 4th hololive member, the 2nd Japanese member, and the 2nd of the third generation to reach this milestone.
