Introduction

Sakura Miko is a virtual YouTuber affiliated with Hololive Production. She debuted on June 1, 2018 as part of the Hololive 1st Generation. Her character design features pink hair, cat ears, and a maid outfit. Sakura Miko is known for her bubbly personality and infectious laughter.

History

Sakura Miko started her career as a virtual YouTuber in 2018. She quickly gained popularity for her funny antics and entertaining streams. She has since become one of the most popular members of Hololive Production. Sakura Miko has also released several songs, including "Kimi no Shiranai Monogatari" and "Night of Summer Bloom"

On 23 December, she revealed her new 3D model, with one of the most notorius changes being that in the new model, her eyes match the redesign Miko received in artworks in 2019 by her new designer, Ordan. Like those of others, to which underwear censorships to prevent potential YouTube bans were applied, her model was updated to wear a pink short under her skirt. Her original appearance model also received a face update.

On 30 April, Miko reached 1,000,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel during a singing endurance stream. This makes her the 14th member of hololive to achieve this milestone, as well as the 11th member of hololive JP. She is also the first one of the Gen 0 to achieve it.

On 7 July, she reached 1,500,000 YouTube subscribers during a singing endurance stream, making her the 12th hololive member to reach this milestone, as well as the 9th member of hololive JP. She is also the second member of the 0th generation to reach it.

Personality

Sakura Miko is known for her upbeat and energetic personality. She loves to make people laugh and often tells jokes during her streams. She also enjoys singing and playing games. Sakura Miko is very close to her fans and often interacts with them during her streams. She is known for her infectious laughter and positive attitude.

Miko is a self-proclaimed "elite" gamer who is somewhat melodramatic and tends to become emotionally attached to NPCs and inanimate objects. Fans tend to use the term "elite" ironically to highlight Miko's questionable decision-making skills as well as her tendency to speak garbled nonsense punctuated by the occasional F-bomb. Though Miko specializes in role-playing games, much of the humor on her channel stems from the fact that even while playing graphic and violent games, such as Grand Theft Auto, she rarely takes her role seriously. Her love for erotic games often finds its way into other games that she plays, especially those featuring freely adjustable camera angles and skirt-wearing female characters. She greets her viewers with "Nya-hello."

Achievements

Sakura Miko has achieved many milestones during her virtual YouTuber career. She has over 1 million subscribers on YouTube and over 400,000 followers on Twitter. She has also released several successful songs and has performed at concerts in Japan and around the world. Sakura Miko is considered one of the most popular and influential virtual YouTubers in the world.

Activities

Sakura Miko is primarily known for her live streams on YouTube. She often streams games such as Minecraft and Apex Legends, as well as singing and dancing performances. Sakura Miko also collaborates with other Hololive Production members and has appeared in a number of virtual concerts. In addition to her online activities, Sakura Miko has released several original songs and merchandise items.

Fanbase

Sakura Miko has a large and dedicated fanbase, known as the "Cherry Blossom Congregation". Her fans appreciate her positive attitude, entertaining content, and willingness to interact with them. Sakura Miko often reads and responds to fan comments during her live streams, and has also held fan meet-and-greet events.
